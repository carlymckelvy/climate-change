Evolution of Atlantic Storms over Time

Atlantic Storm Landfalls

This map shows all storm event landfalls since 1851. The size of the circle is representative of the wind speed at landfall, and the color indicates the decade the event took place. Each event circle can be clicked on to show additional data, such as the storm name, the date of landfall, the wind speed at landfall, the time of landfall, and the status of the storm (TS = Tropical Storm, EX = Extratropical Cyclone, HU = Hurricane, SD = Subtropical Depression (winds < 34t) , SS = Subtropical Storm (winds > 34kt), TD = Tropical Depression). The map can also be dragged and zoomed.
